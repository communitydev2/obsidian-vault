I learned to use humor to defuse other people’s insults. I always assumed that those “insults” came from a good place, deep down. The way I learned to see it, was that they actually did like me, and they were just testing me to see how I would respond. My close friends and I were always teasing one another. We made fun of one another all the time. We didn’t take it personally. If you just laugh things off and use self-deprecating humor – then it communicates to others that you are unattached to what they think of you. Their insults cease to have any power over you.
